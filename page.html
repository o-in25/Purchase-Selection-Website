MEAL PASSES

Just one swipe at Hoff Dining Commons and you can enjoy a full meal or light snack. No need to think about how much you are spending! Enjoy a variety of entrees, sides, soups, salads, cut-fruit, soft serve ice cream, and dessert, all for one swipe. Your meal passes are loaded on your ALL Card on the Saturday before the first day of classes

DINING DOLLARS

Dining Dollars are flexible funds that supplement the meal component of your plan. Consider it “bonus” Meal Credits or extra “food money.”

Buy extra meals or snacks at on-campus retail eateries, vending, Pizza ATM, Hoff Dining Commons, and the All For One Shops.

Buy a meal for a family member or friend when they come to visit.

Dining Dollars roll over from fall to spring semester, but expire on the last day of spring semester.
